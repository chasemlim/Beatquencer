How To Use

Each pad on screen represents a 16th note beat subdivision, with the lighter colored pads representing strong beats. Each color belongs to a different instrument.

Yellow ---- Hi-Hat

Green ---- Toms

Blue ---- Snares & Claps

Red ---- Kick

Click on a pad to activate it, and click again to turn it off. When you press play, Beatquencer will play back the drum pattern you've created. Don't forget to use the clear button if you mess up, and try out the sample beats!
